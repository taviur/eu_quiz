Should I Stay or Should I Go?

The EU referendum is happening on 23rd June 2016, but do we really know whether we should stay in the EU or leave the EU? Sure, there's a ton of information floating around about how leaving or staying would affect the UK, but it's all GDP this and £1.8bn that - but what does it actually mean for our day to day lives? Well we've tried our hardest to bring to light the concrete, real life issues that we would notice a change in in our everyday lives & formulated it into a handy little quiz to help you identify which decision could possibly the right one for you. Take 'Should I Stay or Should I Go' and have a whale of a time!
